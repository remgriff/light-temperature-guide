💡 Pro Tips for Buying LEDs

⭐ Mix temperatures: 2700K in bedroom/living room + 4000K in kitchen/bathroom is the classic home combo.
⭐ Check CRI (Color Rendering Index): aim for CRI 90+ so colours look natural and accurate under the light.
⭐ Dimmable bulbs: a dimmable 2700K bulb gets even warmer when dimmed, perfect for evenings.
⭐ Sleep hygiene: avoid 5000K+ bulbs in rooms you use 2 hours before bed — blue-rich light suppresses melatonin.
⭐ Lumens, not Watts: for LED, look at lumens for brightness. ~800lm ≈ old 60W bulb; ~450lm ≈ old 40W.
